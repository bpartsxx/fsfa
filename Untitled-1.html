FEDERATION OF SOCCER
& FUTSAL ASSOCIATION






FSFA(Federation of Soccer and Futsal Association) is a non-profit organization that describes itself as a federation associated with football and futsal. FSFA was founded in 2018 with a clear vision to support and promote excellent football players, by providing them a league tournament platform to showcase their potential and also get recognized for their talent.

FSFA outlines a number of objectives in the society, providing efforts to ensure that it is accessible to everyone, and advocating for integrity and fair play. FSFA is responsible for the organization and promotion of football tournaments.



AIM:

The main aim of FSFA is to improve and popularize the game of football constantly and promote it throughout the country in the light of its unifying, educational, cultural and humanitarian values, particularly through youth development programs in urban and rural areas including Schools, Colleges and Universities which also having a sole focus on development of skills of footballers and futsal players, while focusing on social awareness and also physicial fitness.










FSFA CHAMPIONS LEAGUE

The FSFA Champions League (abbreviated as FCL) is an annual club football competition organized by the Federation of Soccer & Futsal Association (FSFA) and contested by the best clubs. This will be one of the most prestigious football tournaments in West Bengal and the most prestigious club competition in futsal & football.

Introduced in 2020, as FSFA Champions League. In its present format, the Champions League begins in mid December, The 16 teams are drawn into four groups of four teams each and play with each other in a double round-robin system. The four group winners proceed to the knockout phase that culminates with the final match in late January or early February. The winner of the Champions League take the FSFA-SUPER Cup.
